Institution Overview:

Adarsha School Narayanganj, established and managed by the Islamic Society of Narayanganj, is a prominent Islamic missionary educational institution in Bangladesh. It was founded on January 14, 1975, with the aim of creating devout, competent, honest, patriotic, and good citizens.

Principal’s Message:

The principal expresses a strong commitment to advancing students in all areas, including patriotism, religious values, ethics, education, culture, and sports. The school aims to reach the pinnacle of success in every educational stage with the support of talented teachers and a dedicated managing committee.



Vice principle's message:

The school was established by a group of philanthropists in Narayanganj who envisioned creating an institution that would produce patriotic, honest, just, and dedicated human resources to advance the nation.
